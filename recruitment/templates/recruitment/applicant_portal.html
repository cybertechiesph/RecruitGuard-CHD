{# ── Privacy & Data Protection (RA 10173 assurance) ───────────── #}
Privacy & Data Protection

Your application is private.

We follow the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173). The information you give is used only to process your application. We never share it with anyone else unless you allow us to in writing.

  • Only the hiring team can open it

    Only the recruitment officers and the selection board can see your application and the documents you upload.

  • Used only for this job

    Your information is used only to check if you qualify for the position you applied for — nothing else.

  • Every action is recorded

    Everything done with your application is saved in a secure record, so it can always be checked.

  • We keep you updated

    We send email confirmations and one-time codes to keep your account safe, and we let you know your status at every step.

{# ── Your rights & data retention (R.A. 10173) ───────────── #}

Your rights over your information

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173), you have the right to:

  • Access — ask for a copy of the information we have about you.
  • Correction — ask us to fix wrong or outdated details.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your information, unless we are required to keep it for legal or audit reasons.
  • Withdraw consent — tell us to stop using your information at any time.

To use any of these rights, contact the HRMPSB Secretariat / Data Protection Officer of DOH–CHD CALABARZON.

How long we keep your information

We keep your information only for as long as we need it for recruitment, following R.A. 10173.

If you are hired, your records become part of your official 201 (personnel) file and are kept under the rules of DOH–CHD CALABARZON, the Civil Service Commission, and the National Archives of the Philippines. If you are not hired, your records are kept safely only for as long as needed for audit and re-posting, then securely deleted or made anonymous.
